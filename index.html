Our 15-Day Process

Days 1-3: Consultation & Wireframing

We meet to understand your idea, refine it, and create wireframes outlining the user experience.

Days 4-7: Design & Prototyping

Our design team works on high-fidelity prototypes, ensuring a smooth and intuitive design for both Android and iOS.

Days 8-12: Development

We develop your MVP using cross-platform frameworks like Flutter or React Native, ensuring rapid deployment on both platforms.

Days 13-15: Testing & Delivery

Final testing, tweaks, and app store preparations. By the end of Day 15, your MVP is ready for launch!
